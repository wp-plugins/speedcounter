A simple visitor counter for your Blog.
No Javascript required & looks pretty on retina as well!

== Description ==
[SpeedCounter](https://speedcounter.net/) is a free web counter for your Blog.


It is very simple and will show how many visitors you had today, yesterday, in total and even how many people are on your Blog right now.


It does not require Javascript or CSS and ist completely free of advertising - it is just a image. **No registration needed!**


The counter is widget plugin which you can add in your sidebar or other areas on your Blog. You will also be able to choose from 10 colors. 


The counter image is vector based, meaning it will scale with the screen size ensuring high quality on retina displays or mobile & tablet devices.


This widget is fully compatible with SSL/HTTPS Blogs.

== Installation ==
1. Download, install, and activate SpeedCounter.
2. Drop & drop the widget anywhere in your theme.
3. Optionally select color and do further configuration.
